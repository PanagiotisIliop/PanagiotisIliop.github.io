About me

Panagiotis Georgios Iliopoulos was born in Athens, Greece and raised in the small city of Kalamata, located in the southern Peloponnese region near the sea. From a young age, he developed a passion for sailing and the sea. His aptitude in math and physics enabled him to excel on the Greek SATs, leading him to pursue a degree in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA). Through this decision, he was able to merge his love of science and the sea in his studies and a fulfilling career.
